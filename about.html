About the eConsent System

The Electronic Consent (eConsent) Application is a digital platform designed to simplify and secure the process of obtaining and managing consent in clinical and organizational workflows. It eliminates the need for paper-based consent forms by providing an easy-to-use electronic interface for both patients and administrators.

Our Mission

To streamline consent management by offering a secure, transparent, and efficient digital solution that enhances compliance and patient experience.

Key Features

  • Role-based access and user management
  • Custom form builder for flexible consent templates
  • Integrated electronic signature verification
  • Comprehensive audit trail for accountability
  • Smart notification system for updates and approvals
